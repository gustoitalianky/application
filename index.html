Employee Policy & Conduct Agreement

By accepting employment at Gusto Italian, you agree to read, understand, and abide by the policies set out in this document. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

1. Hiring Requirements
  • Complete and sign all employment application forms prior to your first shift.
  • Provide valid identification and any required employment documents.
  • Complete all required training before working independently.
  • Sign and return your contract within 48 hours of receipt.
2. Dress Code - All Black
  • Black polo shirt - clean, no logos.
  • Black trousers/pants - smart, no rips.
  • Black closed-toe, non-slip shoes.
  • Hair tied back if longer than shoulder length.
  • Clean, neat personal appearance at all times.
3. Work Schedule & Attendance
  • A minimum of 4 shifts per week is required.
  • Arrive at least 10 minutes before your scheduled shift.
  • 3 late arrivals will result in termination - no exceptions.
  • A no-call/no-show is grounds for immediate termination.
4. Personal Phone Use
  • Personal phones must not be used on the floor during your shift.
5. Customer Service
  • Treat all customers with courtesy, professionalism, and respect at all times.
6. Workplace Conduct
  • Follow all reasonable instructions from management.
  • No drugs or alcohol while on duty. Reporting to work under the influence is grounds for immediate termination.
  • Zero tolerance for theft, fraud, unauthorised discounts, fighting, threats, harassment, or discrimination.
7. Hygiene, Health & Safety
  • Wash hands regularly, especially after handling food or cash.
  • Keep your work area clean and organised before leaving.
8. Cash Handling
  • Follow all cash register and payment procedures as directed by management.
9. Grounds for Immediate Termination
  • Theft, fraud, or dishonesty of any kind
  • Violence, threats, harassment, or discrimination
  • Reporting to work under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • No-call/no-show
10. Employee Acknowledgement

I confirm that I have read and fully understood the Gusto Italian Employee Policy and agree to comply with all rules, standards, and expectations contained herein.
